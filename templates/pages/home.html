About Skepticamp

Science. Curiosity.
Community.

Skepticamp NYC is a free, community-organized event for people who love asking questions. Anyone can present — no credentials, no gatekeeping. Just a microphone and an audience eager to learn.

We've been gathering since 2009. Every year brings new voices, new topics, and the same spirit of open-minded inquiry.
