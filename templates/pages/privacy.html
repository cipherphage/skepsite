Legal

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

New York City Skeptics ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Skepticamp NYC registration website at skepticampnyc.org. This privacy policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, and your rights regarding that data.

We take your privacy seriously. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with third parties for marketing purposes. We collect only what we need to organize the event.

What Data We Collect

Category Data Points Purpose Retention
Registration Name, email, phone (optional), registration type, attendance format Event management, confirmation emails, capacity planning 3 years after the event
Presenter info Talk title, description, bio, AV needs Schedule planning, program production 3 years after the event
Accessibility Dietary needs, accessibility requirements Event logistics, accessibility accommodations Deleted after the event
Technical IP address (at registration time) Spam prevention, security 90 days
Preferences Theme preference (localStorage only) Improve your experience Until you clear your browser data

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your registration data under the legal basis of contract: you provide your information to register for an event, and we need it to fulfill that registration. We also rely on your explicit consent (the GDPR checkbox during registration) for sending confirmation emails and event communications.

Your Rights

Under GDPR and CCPA, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability (receive your data in a common format)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at admin@skepticampnyc.org. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies

We use the following cookies and browser storage:

  • skepcamp_theme (localStorage): Stores your light/dark mode preference. Never transmitted to our servers. You can opt out via the theme toggle.
  • skepcamp_cookie_consent (localStorage): Stores your cookie consent decision. Required for this banner to function.
  • Django session cookie: Required for the registration form to function. Expires when you close your browser.
  • Django CSRF token: Required for form security. Not used for tracking.

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies without your explicit consent. If you decline cookies in the consent banner, no analytics or non-essential cookies will be set.

Data Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. We use industry-standard PostgreSQL with encryption at rest. Access to the database is restricted to authorized administrators with multi-factor authentication. We conduct regular security reviews.
